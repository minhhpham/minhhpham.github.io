Credit Card Approval Chance App
​

I am one of those people who apply for credit cards to get rewards. However, we should carefully consider the decision to apply for any credit card. If we get denied, our credits get hurt by an additional hard inquiry while no benefit is gained.
This app uses data collected on Reddit to estimate your chance of credit card approval using statistical & machine learning models.
Discussion of this app on Reddit: ​https://www.reddit.com/r/churning/comments/6oiq2e/credit_card_approvaldenial_app/
Link to app (running on AWS EC2)

Navy Physical Readiness Training

​In April 2018, I started challenging my physicality according to the Navy's Physical Readiness Test: run 1.5 miles as fast as possible, do as many push-ups as possible in 2 minutes, and do as many sit-ups as possible in 2 minutes.
My goal is to run 1.5 miles in under 10:00 minutes and do 80 push-ups and 80 sit-ups in 2 minutes. This would be categorized as Excellent for a sailor after boot camp.
No, I don't plan to join the Navy. The primary purpose of this project is to constantly put my mentality in the state of breaking my own limits. I try to start everyday by breaking my yesterday's record by 1 second in the run, by 1 more push-up, and by 1 more sit-up. Then, I can move on to other things in the day with the limit-breaking mindset. The secondary purpose is, obviously, to stay healthy.
The charts to the right are my own records, updated as soon as I finish the training.
