Credit Card Approval Chance App

I am one of those people who apply for credit cards to get rewards. However, we should carefully consider the decision to apply for any credit card. If we get denied, our credits get hurt by an additional hard inquiry while no benefit is gained.
This app uses data collected on Reddit to estimate your chance of credit card approval using statistical & machine learning models.
